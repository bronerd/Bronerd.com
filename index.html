Hello

My name's Taylor.

I am a Web, UI and Branding designer from Richmond, VA who spends most of his time in search of inspiration. The art of intuitive design, and its ever growing presence in technology, has allowed me to marry my two passions, art and technology. My ambition and creativity helps me to create, build and innovate with the hope of one day changing the world's perspective. Then again, what 23 year old doesn't want to change the world! I am a firm believer in making every moment count, both on a professional and personal level. After all, 'You can only live once, but if you do it right, once is enough.' Thanks for stopping by my site.. if you want to know more about me, shoot me a message.
