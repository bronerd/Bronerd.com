To the Bottom and Back ( 2BNB )

Project info
Client: 2BNB Date: Summer 2011
Work Description

2BNB is an organization that focuses on reducing the quantity of drinking and driving incidents and create safer streets for pedestrians and motorists alike and create awareness through education to the dangers of drinking and driving. They do this by operating buses on continuous loops throughout the major arteries of Richmond from 6pm to 3am, Thursday-Saturday. As a part of a team at Shcokoe.com we built 2bnb a mobile vehicle tracking service that would allow their users to see where the buses we at all times. Built for both mobile and web platforms, this service has provided their business an opportunity for more reliable service, a stronger connection to their users, and an outlet for free publicity / advertising. As the lead designer on the project the products branding and user interface were my two main focuses.
