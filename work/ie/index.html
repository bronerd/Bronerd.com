i.e. Startup Competition

Project info
Client: Greater Richmond Chamber Of Commerce Date: March 2013 Check out i.e.
Work Description

i.e. is a collaborative community initiative to discover, support and showcase creativity and innovation in the Richmond region, and amplify it to the World. As a part of this initiative I (as a part of a team at Shockoe.com) created a mobile voting app for i.e.'s anual startup competition. This app was not only a large part of the event's success but it allowed all the particiapting start-ups a chance to become visable to anyone right from their smart devices. I also had the pleasure of creating most all of the print media on for the start up competition, including the tickets, flyers, and posters (some of which can be seen above).
