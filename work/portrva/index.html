Work Description

As a part of a collaborative project under the direction of Eugene P. Trani (former president of Virginia Commonwealth University) and the Greater Richmond Chamber of Commerce, I was allowed the chance to create the first concepts for the community portRVA revamp project. Although these particular designs were just concepts they may very well soon become a part of the image belonging to the great Port of Virginia. Learn more about the Port of Virginia here. I look forward to working with this project more!
